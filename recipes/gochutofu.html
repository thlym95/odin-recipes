Gochujang Tomato Tofu | By PickUpLimes

Description

Super flavorful, spicy, umami and slightly sweet recipe. It is also quick to prepare! Full description, ingredient quantities and instructions available at PickUpLimes website.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Firm tofu
  • Gochujang
  • Garlic
  • Reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • Light brown sugar
  • Cherry tomatoes

Preparation steps:

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a saut√© pan over medium heat and cook the cubed tofu until golden on all sides.
  3. Mix gochujang, minced garlic, soy sauce and sugar in a bowl for the sauce.
  4. When tofu is golden, transfer to a plate. Cook the halved tomatoes on the now empty pan.
  5. Add the sauce and cook for about 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the tofu, then serve with warm rice.

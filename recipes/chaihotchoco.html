Chai Hot Chocolate | By PickUpLimes

Description

Warm, cozy winter drink. Full description, precise ingredient quantities and detailed recipe is available at PickUpLimes page.

Ingredients:

  • Unsweetened plant milk of choice
  • Dairy-free dark chocolate
  • Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • Granulated sugar
  • Fresh ginger
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Cardamom pods
  • Whole cloves
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper

Preparation steps:

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring throughout to avoid burning.
  2. When hot, pour through a sieve and serve in your favorite mugs.
  3. Top with whipped cream, chocolate shavings or cinnamon sticks as desired.

Lavender Honey

Description

We had a lavender bush at home, so this quick recipe is by my grandmother.

Ingredients:

  • Lavender flowers
  • Water
  • Honey

Preparation steps:

  1. Thoroughly wash the lavender flowers.
  2. Boil water in a pan, turn off the heat.
  3. Transfer the warm water into a small jar, fill half the jar, add the lavender flowers. Close the jar when the water is close to room temperature, and let it set for about 24-48h.
  4. After letting it set, fill the jar with honey, mix.
  5. For a stronger lavender flavor, add the flowers to the water right before it boils. Transfer to the jar together.
